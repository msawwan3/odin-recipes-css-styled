Banana Bread

Quick, easy to prepare and delecious. just make sure you make enough for everyone to have seconds!

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe mashed bananas
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 F or 165 C. Grease 9x5-inch loaf pan
  2. combine bananas, sugar, egg, and butter together. in a seperate bowl, mix flour and baking soda then stir into banana mixture until batter is just mixed. add the salt and stir, then pour into the pan
  3. BAKE! Bread is done when a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. this would take about an hour
