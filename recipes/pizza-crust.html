Pizza Crust

Delicious pizza crust! easy to make and you probably already have all the ingredients you need

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 0.25 ounce active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 ½ cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 F (230 C), grease a pizza pan lightly
  2. place warm water in a bowl, add sugar and yeast. mix and let stand for 10 minutes
  3. add flour, salt, and oil to the yeast mixture. beat until smooth. let rest for 5 minutes
  4. turn dough onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to the prepared pizza pan.
  5. Spread crust and here you will add the sauce of your choice along with whatever toppings you like
  6. Bake until golden brown which would be about 15-20 minutes. remove and let cool for couple minutes and enjoy!
