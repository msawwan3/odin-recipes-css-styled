Muffins

Easy and basic muffins. following these steps you can make your own variation of muffins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F (200 c). Grease 12-cup muffin or use paper liners
  2. stir flour, baking pwder, salt and sugar and make a well in the center
  3. beat egg with fork in a different bowl, whisk in milk and oil. pour into the flour mixture and mix quickly
  4. bake until tops spring back when lightly pressed, thats about 25 minutes
